Basic Gantt Chart
This is a basic Gantt chart demo that displays tasks schedule in weeks and days.
You can drag-and-drop and resize the task bars to change the start/end time and duration of the tasks. Also, you can double-click on the task bar or on corresponding grid row to call a lightbox with form to edit the task details. Click on “+” sign to create a new child task.
